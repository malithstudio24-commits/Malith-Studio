Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 03, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Malith Studio®. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our tools. By using our site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways to provide and improve our services:

  • Comments Section: When you leave a comment, we collect the name and message you provide. This information is stored in our secure Firebase database to be displayed publicly on the website.
  • AI-Powered Tools: For our AI tools (Text Summarizer, Presentation Idea Generator, Image to Text), the text or images you provide are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. We do not store this input data on our servers. Your interaction with these tools is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • To-Do List Tool: The tasks you create in the To-Do List are stored locally in your browser's `localStorage`. This data is completely private to you and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Resume Builder: All data you enter into the Resume Builder, including personal details, work history, and your photo, is processed and stored entirely within your web browser. This information is never sent to our servers or any third party, ensuring your complete privacy. The generated PDF is created directly on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and maintain our website, display user-generated content (comments), and provide the functionality of our tools. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website relies on the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AI (Gemini): Powers our AI tools. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: Used for our real-time comments database. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our website. GitHub Privacy Statement.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
