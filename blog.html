Announcing Our Newest Tool: The Resume Builder!

Published on September 3, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the latest addition to the Malith Studio® family of tools: the Resume Builder! Creating a professional resume can be a challenging task, so we've built a tool that simplifies the entire process, allowing you to create a polished, effective CV in just a few minutes.

Our new Resume Builder is designed with you in mind. It features a clean, step-by-step interface, modern and classic templates to choose from, and the ability to add a professional photo. Most importantly, your privacy is our priority. All the information you enter is processed directly in your browser and is never sent to our servers. Your data remains your own, from start to finish.

Ready to create your perfect resume? Give the new tool a try and let us know what you think in the comments section on our homepage. We are always working to improve and your feedback is invaluable to us. Happy building!

The Journey Begins: The Story of Malith Studio

Published on August 31, 2025

Welcome to the official blog of Malith Studio®! Our journey started with a simple idea: to create powerful, easy-to-use digital tools that save time for busy people. In a world filled with complex software, we wanted to build solutions that are both intuitive and efficient.

Our first tool, the Voice Typing application, was born out of this desire. From there, we expanded our collection to include a range of productivity tools, each designed to tackle a specific daily challenge. This website is the home for all our creations, and we are incredibly excited to share them with you.

Why AI is a Game-Changer for Everyday Productivity

Published on September 5, 2025

Artificial Intelligence is no longer just a buzzword; it's a practical tool that can significantly enhance our daily productivity. At Malith Studio, we've integrated AI into tools like our Text Summarizer and Presentation Idea Generator to automate complex tasks.

Imagine summarizing a long report in seconds or getting a complete presentation outline just by typing a topic. This is the power of AI we are harnessing. It's not about replacing human creativity but augmenting it, allowing you to focus on what truly matters. We believe this is just the beginning, and we're excited to explore more ways AI can simplify our lives.
